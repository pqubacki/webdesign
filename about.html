OUR RESTAURANT

Formerly Golden Tandoori, Sweet 'n' Spice is located on Upper Dorset Street in the centre of Dublin. This Indian takeaway serves a wide range of classic dishes such as Korma, Masala, Jalfrezi and Madras.
You will find us at 64 Dorset Street Upper, Dublin 1

ABOUT US

Three brothers who shared the passion for food and tradition.
We opened our restaurant in 2013 with the idea of sharing
the taste and flavor of famous and delicious Indian cuisine with you in Dublin.
